What is my responsibility?

At Latitude Financial Services, we will not compromise on our ethics or principles. We stand for integrity and are committed to taking action to address any potential breaches of our Code of Conduct.

It is important that you have the courage to challenge actions/decisions that you do not agree with and that you are willing to escalate a concern when you believe the action/decision may pose a risk to our people, property, customers, the environment, sustainable business practices or the reputation of Latitude Financial Services.

How do we support you?

The objective of the Concern Raising Process is to provide all Latitude employees with:

  • An easy way to raise concerns;
  • A means of raising concerns where retribution or retaliation will not be tolerated; and
  • An ability to raise matters anonymously if required.

Who do I raise concern to?

Your concern should be raised to (as appropriate):

  • Your Leader or another Business Leader
  • An HR Representative
  • A Compliance Representative
  • A Legal Representative
  • An EH&S Representative; or
  • An Integrity Leader

How will my concern be addressed?

The person who receives the concern will:

  • Listen carefully to your concern
  • Be fair, objective and impartial
  • Respect your confidentiality (to the greatest extent possible)
  • Take the relevant steps to ensure an appropriate investigation is undertaken; and
  • Provide feedback/advice of closure to you (when not raised anonymously)
